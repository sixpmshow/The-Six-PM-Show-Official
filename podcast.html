About Podcast

Starting as an open mic community back in college, the lockdown blues confined us to our homes, thus leaving us jobless. Nevertheless, we tried to do something creative and absolutely new to keep our audience engaged, and started our very own podcast series "Making Sense With". “Making Sense With” is our attempt.at sharing the stories of such talented people around us who are second to none. From Salil Acharya to Nivedita Lakra, we interviewed a wide variety of people and tried our best to present a quick sneak peak in their world!
