About us
About us
The Six Pm Show is a student run-firm from SRM Institute of Science and Technology. It is “Where art meets stage”. Our mission is to curate the most captivating content and promote talent indifferent to who you are. We hold a variety of events ranging from open mics to live performances for artists of all kinds. Our persistence to quality and hard work has shown through our consistent growth on all platforms and the steady increase in the total turnout of our events.
